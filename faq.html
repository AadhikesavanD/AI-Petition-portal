How do I file a petition?

Go to the "Apply Petition" page, select whether the petition is for yourself or others, fill in the details, and submit.

How can I track my petition status?

Visit the "Track Status" page, enter your Petition ID, complete the CAPTCHA, and click "Track" to see the status.
