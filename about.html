About Us

About the Portal

The Petition Service Portal is designed to help citizens file, track, and resolve grievances efficiently.

How It Works

1. Sign up or log in to your account.
2. File a petition through the "Apply Petition" page.
3. Track your petition status using the Petition ID.
4. Receive updates until resolution.

Do's

- Provide accurate details when filing a petition.
- Use the portal responsibly.
- Check your petition status regularly.

Don'ts

- Do not submit false or misleading information.
- Avoid filing duplicate petitions for the same issue.
- Do not misuse the complaint system.
